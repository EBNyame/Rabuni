About Rabuni Prayer Ministry

The Rabuni Prayer Ministry is dedicated to creating a space where people can experience the transformative power of prayer and the love of Jesus Christ. They are committed to building a prayerful community that supports one another in faith, and spreads the message of hope and salvation through Jesus. Our Mission

Our mission at Rabuni Prayer Ministry is simple yet profound: to empower individuals through prayer and to cultivate a strong, supportive community grounded in the teachings of Jesus Christ. We believe in the power of prayer to bring healing, guidance, and transformation to individuals, families, and our community.

Meet Our Leadership Team

The leadership team at Rabuni Prayer Ministry is passionate about prayer, ministry, and witnessing lives transformed by God's grace. Led by Rev. Maxwell Paul Boateng, the ministry has been blessed with a team of faithful leaders who work collaboratively to guide the church, encourage spiritual growth, and inspire action within the community
